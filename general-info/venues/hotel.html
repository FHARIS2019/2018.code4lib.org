Getting to the Venue

From LAX

Driving Directions

Take the San Diego Freeway (I-405) North and exit at Wilshire Boulevard East. Turn right onto Wilshire Boulevard and proceed a half mile to Westwood Boulevard. Turn left and the UCLA Luskin Conference Center is located one mile ahead at end of the turnaround.

LAX Flyaway Bus

The LAX Flyaway Bus has a drop off location in Westwood, just south of the UCLA campus. The bus departs from LAX on the hour every hour. Once in Westwood, you can walk up Westwood Blvd.

Public Transport

You can take the Culver City Bus line 6R from LAX to UCLA.

Taxis

For rates to and from the airport as well as general taxi info, see the Los Angeles International Airport taxi information page.

See more transportation options on Google Maps.

From Hollywood Burbank (Bob Hope) Airport

Driving Directions

Take Hollywood Way south and get onto California Highway 134 West. Continue onto California Highway 101 North. Take the Interstate-405 South Freeway toward San Diego. Take the Sunset Boulevard exit and turn left onto North Church Lane. Turn left onto Montana Avenue and continue onto Gayley Avenue. Turn left onto Strathmore Place and take the second left onto Westwood Plaza. Destination will be on the left.

Public Transport

You can take Metro lines 164 or 165 from Bob Hope Airport, then transfer to Metro Rapid Line 734, which will take you to UCLA.

Shuttles & Taxis

For rates to and from the airport as well as general taxi info, see the Hollywood Burbank (Bob Hope) Airport taxi information page.
